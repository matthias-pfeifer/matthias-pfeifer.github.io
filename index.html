About Me

I'm a doctoral student in algebraic geometry at TUM working with Christian Liedtke. I will be defending my thesis in fall 2026 and am currently looking for a postdoc position.

My research focuses on deformations of quotient singularities, particularly in positive and mixed characteristic. Further research interests of mine include toric singularities, non-commutative deformation theory and the interplay between infinitesimal and local deformations.
